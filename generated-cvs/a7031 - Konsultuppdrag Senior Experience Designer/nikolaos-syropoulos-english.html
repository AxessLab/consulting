Nikolaos Syropoulos

Senior UX/UI Designer & Product Designer (CPACC)

Nikolaos "Nicko" Syropoulos is a senior Product Designer and UX/UI Designer with more than 12 years of experience designing digital products for web and mobile. He works across the full design process, from user research, discovery, workshops, information architecture and concept development to interaction design, high-fidelity prototyping, usability testing, design systems and implementation support.

Nicko has delivered design work in complex, stakeholder-rich environments for organizations including Zalando, Nordea, CSN, ICA, FUB and public-sector healthcare initiatives. He collaborates closely with product owners, engineers, researchers and business stakeholders, and has experience aligning teams around clear product direction, scalable UX patterns and inclusive customer experiences.

He is CPACC certified and has deep expertise in accessibility, cognitive accessibility and inclusive design. His recent work includes design-system evolution and large-scale accessibility adoption at Zalando, innovation-platform design at Nordea, and end-to-end product design for services used by people with cognitive disabilities.
